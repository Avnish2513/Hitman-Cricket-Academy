Rules and Regulation

  • Coaching will be provided to kids of age 6 to 18 yrs
  • Trainees will have to report in SCA white cricket clothes.
  • Trainees should be punctual on every coaching day.
  • Trainees will bring their own Cricket Kit and protective gears.
  • Trainees must maintain discipline during the practice.
  • Trainees will have to take care of their belongings. SCA shall not be responsible for any loss.
  • First-aid Kit will be provided during the practice session at all centres.
  • The trainee would be registered as per their selected centre only.
